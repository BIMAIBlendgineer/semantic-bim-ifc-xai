# Publication Policy

Semantic XAIBIM publishes only reduced and sanitized research artefacts.

Allowed:

- Public examples.
- Aggregated metrics.
- Reduced schemas.
- Documentation.
- Replay harnesses.
- Validation scripts.

Excluded:

- Private BIM models.
- Raw experiment workspaces.
- Raw model outputs from internal runs.
- Large binary model artefacts.
- Model adapters.
- Training checkpoints.
- Internal logs.
- Local machine paths.
- Credentials.
- Protected normative text copied verbatim.
- Product or certification claims.

The repository is a scientific dissemination layer, not an internal development repository.
